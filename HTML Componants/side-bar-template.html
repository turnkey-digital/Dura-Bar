Continuous Cast Iron & Bronze Alloys

Dura-Bar is an engineered iron that offers machining advantages over carbon and alloy steel as well as other continuous cast iron bars. Dura-Bar continuous cast iron bar stock provides solutions, improves performance and results in signiﬁcant long-term cost savings. Dura-Bar Metal Services also offers a complete line of continu- ous cast and wrought, or extruded, bronze alloys—from standard stock offerings to virtually any special alloy, shape or size. Bronze alloys can be brazed and soldered, are easy to machine and wear well against steel.
