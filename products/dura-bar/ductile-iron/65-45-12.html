65-45-12 Ductile Iron


Dura-Bar 65-45-12 ductile iron contains nodular graphite in a matrix of ferrite with small amounts of pearlite. Our most popular Dura-Bar grade, 65-45-12 ductile iron has excellent machinability with good surface ﬁnishes, optimal impact strengths, fatigue properties, electrical conductivity and high magnetic permeability. This iron has approximately the same tensile and yield strengths as AISI 1020 steel in the as-rolled condition. This speciﬁcation conforms to ASTM A536 grade 65-45-12.

Microstructure

The microstructure consists of Types I & II nodular graphite as deﬁned in ASTM A247. The matrix is ferrite with approximately 5-25% pearlite. The "rim" will have a higher nodular count and will be mostly ferrite. Chill carbides will be less than 5% in any ﬁeld at 100x and will be well dispersed.
